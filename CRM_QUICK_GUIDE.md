ARA CRM QUICK GUIDE


WHAT THIS IS

1. One CRM for an ad agency running Meta and Google ads for multiple clients.
2. Two doors into the same data. Agency staff log in at slash login. Clients and their telecallers log in at slash client login.
3. Everything is live. A lead saved on one side appears on the other within thirty seconds.



PART ONE. AGENCY SIDE


HOW TO LOG IN

1. Open the app and the login page appears.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click Sign In. You land on the Dashboard.


THE LEFT SIDEBAR

1. Dashboard. Today's summary of every client.
2. Daily Lead Data. Day by day lead counts. Now has two views inside.
3. Clients. Add, edit, link Meta and Google ad accounts.
4. Ads Comparison. Side by side performance of every client.
5. Analytics. Charts and graphs for one client.
6. Settings. Your profile.
7. Client Portal. See which clients can log in.
8. Access Management. Add agency users, set roles.


DAILY LEAD DATA. TWO VIEWS

1. By Client. The original table. One row per client per day. Shows Meta Form, WhatsApp, Total Leads, Spend.
2. Lead Check. Day wise rollup across all clients. One row per day. Includes Daily, Weekly, Monthly quick toggle.
3. Pick a range from the toggle or set custom From and To dates.
4. Filter by client to focus on one account.


CLIENT AD DETAILS. FOUR TABS

Open any client from the dashboard or clients list.

1. Google Ads tab. Account info, billing, daily trend chart, campaigns, keywords.
2. Meta Ads tab. KPI grid showing Total Leads, Form Leads, WhatsApp Leads, CPL, Reach, Impressions, Clicks, CTR, CPC, CPM. Leads table below.
3. EOD Report tab. The full daily telecalling snapshot. Inside this tab is a small toggle to switch between Daily EOD and Monthly Abstract.
4. Lead Check tab. Per day Meta Ads metrics for this client. Daily, Weekly, Monthly toggle. One row per day showing Spend, Reach, Impressions, Clicks, CTR, CPC, CPM, Form Leads, WhatsApp, Total Leads, CPL.



PART TWO. CLIENT PORTAL


HOW TO LOG IN

1. Open slash client login.
2. Type username or email and password.
3. Click Sign In. You land on the Dashboard.


THE LEFT SIDEBAR

1. Dashboard. Today's live snapshot.
2. Leads. Full page leads table.
3. EOD Report. Daily snapshot plus Monthly Abstract.
4. Google Ads. Admins only.
5. Meta Ads. Admins only.
6. Users. Admins only.

The sidebar collapses to icons only with the small arrow at the bottom.


ROLE RULES

1. Admin sees every tab.
2. Telecaller sees only Dashboard, Leads, and EOD Report.
3. Add or edit users from the Users tab. Set username, optional email, password, and role.



DASHBOARD. LIVE TODAY VIEW

1. Six big tiles. Total Leads today, Connected, Total Calls, Appointments, Consulted, Conversions.
2. Six source cards. WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Google Lead, Walk In or Referral, Incall.
3. Call Activity box. Fresh calls, Call backs, Connected, Not Connected, with a connect rate bar.
4. Appointments box. Today's count, Booked today, Upcoming five days.
5. Every number is clickable. Click a tile or source card to open the Leads page filtered by that label.
6. The page auto refreshes every thirty seconds. A small green dot near the title shows it is live.



LEADS PAGE. THE WORKING TABLE

1. Lives at slash client portal slash leads.
2. Filter by Source, Call Label, Response, Hair or Skin, Appointment, plus free text search.
3. Date range with Today, 7 Days, 30 Days quick buttons.
4. Click Add Lead to create a manual entry. Pick Source from the dropdown for WhatsApp, Instagram, Walk In and others.
5. Click any row to expand and edit Call Date, Call Label, Response, Remarks, Appointment fields.
6. Click plus to add a follow up call to the same lead.
7. Delete is allowed only for manual entries. Synced Meta form leads cannot be deleted.
8. Excel and PDF export buttons at the top right.



EOD REPORT. THE DAILY SHEET

The page matches the team's existing Google Sheet one to one. Two views inside.


DAILY EOD. FOUR SECTIONS

1. Header. Telecalling Report dash client name, then DATE and the date.
2. Day Target vs Achieved. Consulted Appointment Target and Achieved, Total Calls today, Connected Calls Target and Achieved. Achieved cells highlight in red so a miss stands out.
3. Month Target vs Achieved. Same metrics but for the whole month. Adds a Projection column. Projection percentage highlights in red.
4. Appointment Status. Yesterday, Today, plus the next five days. Total Appointment Booked, Visited, Rescheduled, Not Visited.
5. Day Summary. Three column block. Leads Abstract on the left listing every source. Total Calls in the middle. Total Connected Calls on the right. A Total Appointments Booked tile and a Consultation and Conversion block at the bottom.


EDITABLE TARGETS

1. Click any number under Target in either Day or Month sections to edit it.
2. Type the new target and press Enter to save.
3. The percentage rows recompute right away.
4. Targets save per client.


AUTO REFRESH

1. The EOD page silently re fetches every thirty seconds.
2. A small loading dot near the title appears while a refresh runs.
3. The moment a telecaller saves a lead, response, or follow up, the numbers here update within thirty seconds.



MONTHLY ABSTRACT. THE MONTH GRID

1. Inside the EOD Report tab, switch the toggle to Monthly Abstract.
2. Pick the month from the dropdown.
3. One row per date in the month. Top row shows the month TOTAL.
4. Columns are grouped into four bands.
   a. Source columns. WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Google Lead, Justdial, Walk In, Referral, Physical Marketing, Incall Google, Incall FB, Incall Insta, Incall Self.
   b. Lead status. Total Leads, Connected, Not Connected, Invalid or Duplicate.
   c. Call columns. Fresh Calls, Call Back 1, Call Back 2, Call Back 3, Call Back, Total Calls, Fresh Calls Connected, Call Back Connected, Total Connected Calls.
   d. Appointments and Conversion. Total Appointments, Consulted, Convert, Convert Value.
5. Maroon banner headers and cream rows match the spreadsheet exactly.
6. Use this view for month wise audits and to spot the strongest and weakest days.



HOW THE NUMBERS CONNECT

1. Telecallers add leads or update calls in the Leads table.
2. Those saves write straight to the Lead database.
3. The Dashboard, EOD Report, and Monthly Abstract all pull from that same Lead database.
4. There is one source of truth. No spreadsheets to sync.



COMMON DAY TO DAY TASKS

1. Add a new WhatsApp lead. Go to Leads, click Add Lead, pick WhatsApp from Source, fill name, phone, location. Save.
2. Log a call. Open the lead row, set Call Date, Call Label, Response, Remarks. Save.
3. Book an appointment. Set Appointment Status to APPOINTMENT BOOKED and pick the Appointment Date.
4. Reschedule. Update the same lead, change status to RESCHEDULED, pick a new date.
5. Mark as Consulted. Set Response Label to CONSULTED. The Consulted counter on the dashboard and EOD updates within thirty seconds.
6. Mark as Converted. Set Response Label to TREATMENT BOOKED or CLOSED.
7. Change the day target. Open EOD Report, click the Target number under Day or Month, type a new value, press Enter.
8. Audit the month. Open EOD Report, switch to Monthly Abstract, pick the month.
9. Export today's leads. Open Leads, click Excel or PDF at the top right.



TROUBLESHOOTING

1. Numbers look stale. Click Refresh in the top right of the section, or wait thirty seconds for the auto refresh.
2. Login fails on the client portal. Confirm username is correct (case insensitive) and the user is set to Active in the Users tab.
3. Telecaller cannot see Meta Ads or Google Ads. That is by design. Only admins see those tabs.
4. Reach column shows zero on Lead Check. The backend needs a restart to serve the new aggregation.
5. Login wipes after a moment. Check that the API URL in the frontend env file matches the backend that is actually running.



SECURITY NOTES

1. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt on save.
2. Admin and portal sessions use separate JWT tokens.
3. The portal login accepts a short grace window on a single failed request, then logs you out only if the failure is clearly a token problem.
